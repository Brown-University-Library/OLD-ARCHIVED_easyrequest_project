easyrequest — info

The easyrequest service helps you get the items you need when directly accessing the stacks is not possible.

Here's a typical flow...

  • You'll start in Josiah, the Brown-Library catalog.
  • You'll do a search on Lincoln, and eventually find an item you want. Example: Lincoln, by Rev. A. Clark.
  • If the 'Status' is 'Available', to the right you'll see the link 'Request Item'. Clicking that link gets you to this easyrequest service.
  • After logging in, you'll see a confirmation that your request has been received, and a note that you'll get an email from the Library when your item is ready to be picked up.
  • You'll also receive a confirmation email.

If you have questions, Library staff can help.
